## Strategy

AI PubKit should not start by attacking broad terms like `social media scheduling tool`.
The better wedge is:

1. AI content distribution.
2. Content repurposing.
3. Social media cross-posting.
4. App-specific AI publishing answers.
5. Tool/generator pages once product features exist.

## Product Implication

The SEO/GEO path should lead to a product demo:

- Input one source content item.
- Choose 3-5 destinations.
- Generate app-ready outputs.
- Publish directly to easy APIs first: WordPress, Ghost, Notion, Telegram, Discord, Slack.
- Mark YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X, and China-market apps with conservative assisted/research statuses.
